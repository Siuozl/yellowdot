# "Paralyze needs to be banned", says H1FFIX
###### Written by Snizzle
A discussion in YellowDot has caught a lot of people's attention - The controversy of Paralyze ability.
## What's Paralyze?
It's a Chi-Blocking ability that stops benders from **moving and bending**. Many people think it is too overpowered, but some think it's genuinely good because it's what makes Chi balanced. Without it, what would Chi Blocking be?
## Deeper Dive
In H1FFIX's original post, he says that Paralyze should only be usable by pure Chi-Blockers, as multibenders (Pro, Avatar, Titan, Founder, etc.) can use Chi Blocking *and* bending one after the other, thereby easily killing the other person. However, all bending is always accessible to multibenders, so it would really be weird to just remove it from multibending.
## Reaction from the public
I went ahead and interviewed multiple people about their opinion on this topic. Here's what they said.

### xCursed
> I don't think paralyze is a fair move for Multibenders. It is used to start multiple combos that you can't get out of. It is only balanced with pure Chi-Blockers. So yes, banned.

### Kamigoroshi_
> I say it shouldn't be banned, because even though it's probably overpowered, people should still be able to use it.

### Omniternal
> I think for Multibenders it should be banned because they could easily kill other single benders after one or two uses of the move.

### Additional discussion message from TheMatrix (ThomasFinallyBetter)
> As a Multibender myself, I haven't used Chi Paralyze. But I'm not picking sides or anything, most Multibenders usually use light spirit and dark spirit most of the time.

## Analysis
Opinions are very mixed, and I think that BendersMC won't remove it, even though it might be a good idea. What they could do it just remove Chi from Multibending fully - thereby fixing the whole Paralyze problem.
