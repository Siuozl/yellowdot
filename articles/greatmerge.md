# The Great Merge of BendersMC
In the good old days, there were only 5 servers. Survival1, Survival2, Survival3, Survival4 and Factions. Some people might remember these gamemodes.

## Survival1
This was the server that most people played. They would log on for the first time and just choose number 1, since it's the first. The economy and players were mostly in Survival1. The others weren't known.
## Survival2
If you remember this server, you might recall that it was hated by everyone else. I myself (Snizzle) used to play on S2 and I didn't see the problem with it, other than the fact that some commands were missing and making a playerwarp costed a lot less than on the other servers. I asked around about why it was hated so much. Here's what I got.

## Deep Dive: Why was Survival2 hated?
Some people say that the players on the old S2 were bad at PVP, which is partially true. It just ended up that way randomly, and most good PVPers went elsewhere. Additionally to this, it was way more laggy than the other Survivals. We all know that today, Survival1 is *very* laggy, but we'll talk about that in a little bit. 

The server is said to have been very UCSR-focused. Having been part of both the server and UCSR, I totally agree that it was *the* UCSR hotspot. When the big war started, more hate went towards S2 for this exact reason.

However, many S2 players agree that the community was less toxic than S1. It was easier to make friends on it and to have fun in general.

Turtleman560, a previous player of Survival2 says:
> The lag was kind of annoying but I honestly miss the days of S2, it was smaller and had a good, pretty tight knit community. I was sad when it shut down, and the staff didn't seem prepared for the reset and it was messy for the first 6 months or so.
It was indeed messy. That's one of the following topics. Let's move onto S3 and S4 first.

## Survival3 & Survival4 - Forgotten Servers
Actually alot of people mained on S3 and S4 (Mostly S3), and we often forget about it.

They were liked for being active and friendly. There's not much to say about these other than the fact that they were "good servers" and complementary to S1.
## Survival5 - The addition
After a certain time, Blitz decided to add a 5th survival server. People liked it. It was used often as a replacement for all the other servers, when they'd be down, or just generally as an alternate server to play on. Soon after it was made though, it also left, along with everything other than S1.
---
## The Great Merge
Literally no one was happy about the merge. All those memories, communities, *differences*, all gone. All that was left is one combined server, making it lag a lot more than before. Now, if you're lucky, you might hit 15 FPS on a good day, but on a normal day you sit at 5 FPS (with shaders obviously). BendersMC actually lost most of their players because of the merge. Only around 300 active players remain (300 constant players, so there's thousands of them out there but only 300 at a time).

To fix these problems, BendersMC made a miserable attempt at bringing back what we loved. They remade Survival2 recently, which has just a completely different economy because of how new it is, and only one sixth (approximately) of the community moved to this new server. 

We can't go back, it can't be undone. The Great Merge broke BendersMC. 

All that remains is the ones who stay loyal to them *even though* it's way less fun than it used to be.
