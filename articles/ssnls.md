# Seasonal sets and crates
## What are seasonals?
BendersMC has had crates for a long time. There are:
- Free crate
- Vote crate
- Crystal crate
- Mythic crate
- Bender crate
- Seasonal crate
- **Vault crate**

Seasonal crate keys are priced around 6$ USD. They are the most expensive and rarest **keys** you can have. However, with 2 keys you can buy a "Vault Crate Key". All seasonals that have expired are put in the Vault Crate.

## The history of seasonals and crates
All of the 7 crates used to be like a gambling game: you'd buy a key and then roll for items, get your reward and be sad that you didn't get the one you wanted, so you would buy it again and again and again.
Well, gamble no more! Back when they added Survival5 (a server that is now gone, because they used to have 4 servers, then 5, then 1, now 2), YouTuber "DuperTrooper" got into the server and started duping items and breaking the economy. When he was banned and all of it was over, Blitz (BMC Owner) messaged DuperTrooper, and they made a deal. Turns out DuperTrooper targets servers that encourage kids to gamble. Together, they found a way to make the crates no longer randomized, so people can choose their reward. Today, only the Free, Vote and Vault crate are randomized. By taking Vault, you are CHOOSING to use your seasonal keys to get something older.
## What's the interest of having them?
They're collectibles. Most people with more than 1mil have atleast one seasonal. You can get them through the shop or through the AFK lobby. The longer you keep it, the more valuable it gets. It's a good idea to invest in seasonals. You could wait for one to get double value and then buy 2, and keep doing that... 'till you have 1bil worth of them! And they look good. Like... very good.
