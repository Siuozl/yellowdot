# The first imperial war

 *It has been 2 years since the start of the 1st imperial war. the start of the eternal conflict that has now engulfed the server and is not likely to ever end. here is how that ancient war began.*
In April of 2024 there was a new united groups server. There was the old and ancient empires, and new ones, there was the elemental empire and the elemental clan, sky city and Ragnarok. The clan called CS had been destroyed by a collision, the water tribe and fire nations had died in the reset and the kingdom of the red sea had long fallen into irrelevancy. But there were new nations. The golden gladiator's empire was one of them. But there was a new super power, the UCSR.

The UCSR had been rising to power for the last few months prior to April and they proclaimed the stormpoint accords, the Monroe doctrine for survival 4,

## THE STORMPOINT ACCORDS
- No nation or organization resembling one whose home server is different from Survival Four will establish a base, town, outpost or any such thing on Survival Four.

- Any nation in violation of Article 1 will be announced as an enemy of the Union of Communist Survival Republics.

- Any nation with land already on Survival Four that violates Article 1 will be granted 2 weeks to remove it and potential reparations from the Union of Communist Survival Republics too be paid on different servers of the nations choosing.

- The Union of Communist Survival Republics will not expand onto Factions, S1 or S2 for as long as this remains active.

As the reset has just happened, I doubt any nation is too attached to S4 other than us and native teams. Thanks guys, @me or smth if you have questions.

The UCSR had defeated all the other nations of survival 4, conquering jungle port, port city, earth town, water town, an air settlement, and water town, and forcing them to join them in the finalization war in the first few days of April. The UCSR had conquered the whole of survival 4 and because of them, survival 4 was now the most popular survival instead of survival 4 The UCSR was constantly at war and they had by far the best army in the whole server known as the red army.

After survival 2-4 did a soft reset, the elemental empire built a base on a hilltop on survival 4, attempting to establish a presence survival 4. The ruler of the UCSR, sageplasma444, took issue with this and issued an ultimatum to the elemental empire, demanding that they leave their base in exchange for money on all the other survivals. The elemental empire promptly rejected that offer and so the UCSR declared war on the elemental empire on the 4th of april in the name of anti-imperialism, so it was called the imperial war.
[!](https://siuozl.github.io/yellowdot/articles/images/first-imp-war/image1.png)
The red army attacked the elemental empire's base, camping and destroyed what they could, killing any civilians that stepped outside of a claim. The elemental empire hardly ever showed up to battle them, when they did it was only a few fighters who would get slaughtered by the red army's better skill and superior numbers. Most of the elemental empire's base was fine for the most part but there was noticeable damage to some buildings and outside the base and claim. The UCSR also tried something new, they deployed the red navy, they built multiple ships in the Elemental empire's harbor, they operated as supply depots and as an ever-present sight that the red army was there. The occupation lasted about 2 weeks.

On the 6th of April the united factions made an announcement about the war.
## United Factions News!

> Elemental Empire and USCR at war! 🔥 ⚔️
> The USCR has invaded the Elemental Empire after the failure of the Elemental Empire to follow the rip off monroe doctrine on S4, despite its "vagueness". The Elemental empire sent a demand for 40million which was obviously denied by sage, who sent a counter ultimatum. Of which master declined. Afterwards sage declared war. Revamped Factions came out almost two weeks ago! If you somehow still havent tried it out; do so.

> A more detailed news will be sent tomorrow at whenever the ####. - Sincerely Aleutian
Secret saw this announcement, followed by the screenshots above and asked a question in the united factions general. So on the 7th secret asked if the communists won the war, and the leader of the UCSR, sageplasma444 responded saying no and that secret should join them. Secret and sage has a long conversation in which sage asked for secret and elementum to become a CSR for the UCSR, a csr is a state of the UCSR that has autonomy to govern themselves. Secret said he'd get back to him on it, but in reality, the chance that they join was not likely, the UCSR was on survival 4 and 3 and not at all on survival 1, so it didn't much effect elementum. This was the first time secret meet sage.
On the 23rd the elemental empire admitted defeat in the war. They signed the treaty of eviellan which ended the war and made the elemental empire pay war reparations to the UCSR as well as forbid them from expanding on survival 4. This was the end of the 1st imperial war.
