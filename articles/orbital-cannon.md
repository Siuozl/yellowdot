# Orbital Strike Cannon — A New Landmark in the Making

A thrilling new project is underway on the BendersMC server, and it's already turning heads across the community. Player **judehigh213** — also known by their nickname **Judelow** — has begun construction of what is called an **"Orbital Strike Cannon"**, a towering and highly complex build located in **Survival 1**.

---

## An Engineering Feat

The structure, still incomplete, showcases an incredibly intricate design, with:
- Advanced **redstone circuitry**
- **Layered mechanisms** and carefully engineered components

...that highlight just how ambitious the project truly is.

## Community Reaction

The project has quickly sparked a **mix of excitement and concern** among players:

> *"Many are impressed by the sheer scale and technical ambition of the build — especially the advanced redstone mechanisms involved."*
