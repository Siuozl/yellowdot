# Addressing the situation.
## This is my (Snizzle's) quick apology letter.
I created YellowDot hoping it could go alongside BMCN as **partners** but then I made two fatal mistakes. I put in the advertisement that "unlike BMCN, we focus on *your* stories. What I meant to say was that some of our articles are about debates and discussions that happen in the BMCYD server. I didn't mean that BMCN didn't listen to their people, and I think that was extremely misunderstood, but understandably. 

I can't undo what I said before. However, I can tell you that my intension was **not** to bash BMCN. I like the BMCN, I find them very good, and I was trying to make a complementary newsletter to BMCN, not an enemy. And of course, without thinking, out of excitement, I sent the advertisement into self-promo on BMCN. I totally messed up. I'm so so so so sorry and it's not what I meant.

I hope BMCN can forgive me for what I did, and I hope we can work alongside eachother, **not against eachother**.
