# Minecraft Live March 2026
![image](https://siuozl.github.io/yellowdot/articles/images/mlive-march2026/cave.png)
A new cave biome is being added to our **already magnificent 1.18 caves**. This is such exciting news!

These caves are described as having warm colors and are completely different from any caves they've made before.

![image](https://siuozl.github.io/yellowdot/articles/images/mlive-march2026/cave1.png)
They feature a new block called "Sulfur", which was leaked a few days prior to the livestream. It's vibrant yellow, similar to gold. Along with that, there is a new **red** block called "Cinnabar". These two are placed in patches all over the caves, making your experience more immersive and fun.
![image](https://siuozl.github.io/yellowdot/articles/images/mlive-march2026/cave2.png)

## A toxic swimming pool
![image](https://siuozl.github.io/yellowdot/articles/images/mlive-march2026/water-pools.png)
Across the caves lie patches of water, in a green toxic color. These are caused by the new "Potent Sulfur" block, which makes all surrounding water produce "Noxious Gas". Swimming in these pools will give the player **nausea**. They create an awesome gassy environment, a new type of place to explore. You may sometimes see waterfalls with this green water, resembling the following image.
![image](https://siuozl.github.io/yellowdot/articles/images/mlive-march2026/waterfalls.png)
Beautiful, right? I find it awesome.

Additional to the good looks in caves, the new blocks give us more possibilities in building.
![image](https://siuozl.github.io/yellowdot/articles/images/mlive-march2026/sulfur-in-builds.png)

## New Mob!
In these ominous caves, a new mob will roam around for you to find it. It's called the "Sulfur Cube"!
![image](https://siuozl.github.io/yellowdot/articles/images/mlive-march2026/new-cubes.png)
![image](https://siuozl.github.io/yellowdot/articles/images/mlive-march2026/new-cubes1.png)
Agnes claims that the creature is
> Unlike anything we've seen before.

And I agree. It's so cool that they're experimenting with these new concepts. 

The Sulfur Cube will absorb any block you feed it. These blocks will give it special properties. Then, the cube can be used... as a **ball**. Yes, a ball. You can punch it around and have fun! It will not take any damage when you hit it, which gives us endless possibilities. Just punch it and it will take off.

Blocks like ice will make it slippery, whilst blocks like wood will make it bouncy, and blocks like stone will prevent it from moving upwards. Its AI is removed when you put a block in it, and to take it back out, you can shear it. Additionally, you can split these slimy radiative-looking creatures into two, like a slime or magma cube. However, contrary to the slime, the Sulfur Cube will regrow to full size, letting you duplicate them infinitely. 

To throw them further, simply use a tool that does more damage, because for Sulfur Cubes, damage = distance.

These new balls let us make all of our favourite sports and games, and even traps! Mojang claims that they
> Want the players to make their own experiences.

## They tricked the community, and now it's about to go viral.
There was a ton of speculation beforehand that it would **finally** be the Deep Dark Dimension update, and Mojang almost definetly did it on purpose:

They showed a deep dark and zoomed in on the portal, they activated the portal, and then it just said "Minecraft Dungeons II", instead of the new dimension. For Dungeons fans, it's good news, but for the vast majority of Minecraft players, it's extremely disappointing. They totally tricked us.

## Aftershow with Camman18
Camman18, a YouTube shorts Minecraft content creator, had already announced beforehand that he would be in the aftershow, but it was still extremely funny. He has been trying to get on there for a **LONG** time and finally succeeded. 

He had a competition against Agnes and Jens, workers at Minecraft. 
Agnes won the first game called "Cube Dash", where you had to push a Sulfur Cube down into a hole.
Camman18 won the second game called "The Aim Game", where you had to shoot cubes into holes.
Camman18 and Jens won the third game called "Cube Strikers" where they were in a 2v2, for which Vu Bui joined them in (Vu and Agnes vs Camman18 and Jens). This game was just soccer.
