# The BendersMC "Shamrock Founder" Controversy

The rank of **Founder** has long been the highest purchasable tier on BendersMC. Recently, however, the introduction of a new, higher rank called **Shamrock Founder** sparked significant backlash. Many players view the addition as a "cash-grab" designed to maximize profits. 

### The Developer's Defense
In response to the criticism, an announcement was posted in the *#founder-announcements* channel. The recurring theme was a firm denial of the cash-grab claims. The developers argued that the new perks could actually cause the server to lose money over time due to the value of the free monthly keys included with the rank. 

They justified the real-money cost of the Shamrock upgrade (as opposed to using in-game Lotus Coins) by pointing to the **Shamrock Roll**, which can reward players with seasonal keys. One section of the announcement stated:
> “For those of you saying this is a cash grab — it isn't. We are adding perks that, in the long run, we lose money on.”

### Financial Inconsistencies
Parts of the announcement appear inconsistent when examined closely. The developers claimed: 
*“If you have just regular Founder, then the server loses $71.88 per Founder per year because of the free keys you get.”* However, this is difficult to reconcile with the current pricing structure:

| Item | Calculation | Total |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Founder Subscription** | $19.99 × 12 months | **$239.88** |
| **Seasonal Key Value** | $5.99 × 12 months | **($71.88)** |
| **Net Annual Revenue** | $239.88 - $71.88 | **$168.00** |

Based on this math, the server still generates **$168.00 in yearly revenue** per Founder subscriber. Furthermore, critics point out two major flaws in the "loss" argument:
1. **Zero Marginal Cost:** Keys are digital items; they have no direct production cost, meaning their distribution does not cost the developers physical money.
2. **Server Resets:** Minecraft servers frequently wipe progress, which can negate the long-term value of these perks, turning "permanent" rewards into temporary ones.

### The Shamrock Upgrade
The Shamrock Founder upgrade costs existing members slightly over $10. While the developers claim the cost is due to the **Shamrock Roll**, the odds suggest the house usually wins:
* **Max Reward:** 20% chance for a seasonal key (~$5.99 value).
* **Common Rewards:** Most outcomes range between $1.00 and $3.50 in digital value.

### Community Sentiment
Player feedback remains mixed. While some find the new rank appealing, many argue it contributes to a **pay-to-win (P2W)** environment. Critics suggest that if the goal were truly to prioritize player experience over revenue, powerful features like **multi-bending** would be accessible to the entire community rather than being locked behind expensive, premium ranks.
