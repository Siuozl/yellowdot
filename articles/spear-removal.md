# Should the Spear be REMOVED from BendersMC?

Minecraft's Update 1.21.11 brought in many new mechanics and never-seen before content. However, the main focus on everyone's mind was the new weapon that was added: **The Spear**. The spear worked differently to other weapons in the game, operating on the statistic of speed. The faster you were going, the more damage the spear did. As such, the damage capabilites with it were boundless, but in Player vs. Player combat (PVP), strong usage was limited to those who possessed an elytra. Of course, that was only in the main, *vanilla* game.

## BendersMC


---


On a Minecraft Server like **BendersMC**, where players are able to use the 4 elements to their advantage, you do not exactly need an elytra to travel quickly. In fact, moves such as *AirJets* and *FireJet* allow their user to soar through the skies at breakneck speeds. Combine this easy access to overpowered movement abilites with the spear's simple crafting recipe, and it has the potential to absolutely dominate PVP with almost no effort. As such, many players in the **BendersMC** community have begun to raise questions on whether such a powerful and easy form of combat should be permitted.

#### Conclusion
---
As seen in the interviews, the community is **split** down the middle. While some believe the spear should be removed because it doesn't make use of **bending** while having a large damage output, others bring up the lack of discussion pertaining to weapons like the **mace**, and would choose to keep the spear if given the choice due to the capabilities it has when used alongside the very same bending. Regardless of what is done, there is a good chance that the *spear* is *here to stay*.
